How long this Loving Kindness and Gratitude shield will travel the world?

Each person, regardless of their origin or beliefs, will share the Loving Kindness and Gratitude shield with other person within 8 hours as an act of kindness.

Hence the shield will continue to travel indefinitely across the whole world.

Dos :

Kindly spread the Loving Kindness and Gratitude shield within 8 hours of receiving it without obstructing the shield flow activity in chain link.

One can pass the shield to anyone, irrespective of age group (children, elders, handicapped, etc.),religion, race, nationality etc.

You can share it a maximum of two times with the same person.

One can share the shield with the same person twice if you d like, but please avoid sharing it repeatedly with the same individual.

Kindly help the shield to flow indefinitely throughout the lifetime by sharing the shield expressing loving kindness and gratitude

Rules and Regulations of Loving kindness and Gratitude chain-link Activity?
 
Don'ts:

Don t put the Loving Kindness and Gratitude shield in a decorative vault obstructing the chain flow of Spreading Loving kindness and Gratitude.

One should not break the shield. If you find any damage, kindly help by repairing the shield.

Please avoid sharing the Loving kindness and gratitude shield to same individual more than twice.

Kindly, do not keep the shield with yourself at any moment in your life, obstructing the flow of the Loving Kindness and Gratitude shield chain.

Do not use the loving kindness and gratitude chain for personal gain or to manipulate others.
